FAQ's

How far away are you from the beach?

One of the main entries to Sunshine Beach is just across the road, providing direct access to the surf patrolled area of the beach.

Is Sunshine Beach surf patrolled?

Sunshine Beach is patrolled by lifeguards 365 days a year. Always swim between the flags and speak to one of the friendly lifeguards if you have any questions about the surf.

Does every apartment have an ocean view?

All of Sunshine Beach resort's onsite apartments have great ocean views capturing the sun all year round with a north east aspect.
